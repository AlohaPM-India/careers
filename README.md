👋 **Welcome to AlohaPM India**
Welcome to the official Careers repository of AlohaPM India Pvt. Ltd.

AlohaPM India is the product engineering and technology development center for Aloha Practice Management Inc, a California-based healthcare technology company.

Our engineers collaborate with global teams to design, build, and maintain products used by healthcare organizations across the United States.
